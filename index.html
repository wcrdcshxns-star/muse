Founder & Editor

Premanjan Pradhan
PGT English TeacherMA English LiteratureEditorJharsuguda, OdishaOdia • English • Hindi

Born and raised in Jharsuguda, Odisha, Premanjan Pradhan has spent his career at the intersection of education and literary expression. An accomplished Post Graduate Teacher (PGT) of English, he holds an MA in English Language and Literature from Sambalpur University and a B.Ed. from the Central University of Jharkhand. As both a practising teacher and a passionate educator, he understands exactly what young writers need, focusing on communicative competence and critical thinking.

Words & Visions grew from a simple belief — that a student in a small-town school in Odisha deserves the same platform as one from an elite city school.

"Every student who picks up a pen to write a poem is already a poet. My job is simply to give that poem a home."

Submission Rules

Submission Guidelines

Last updated: March 2025

Word & Length Limits

  • Poems: Maximum 100 lines.
  • Short Stories: 300–2,500 words.

Allowed Themes

Nature, love and loss, identity, patriotism, spirituality, family, humour, and coming-of-age. No violent, sexually explicit, hateful, or politically inflammatory content.

Originality & Plagiarism Policy

All submissions must be original. Plagiarism results in immediate rejection.

AI Usage Policy

We do not accept AI-written content. AI-generated submissions are rejected without refund.

Editorial Rights

The editor may make minor formatting corrections. Substantive edits will be communicated to the author.

Publishing Contribution Policy

₹500 for poems, ₹1,000 for stories. Non-refundable once editorial review begins. Full refund issued within 7 working days for platform errors.

Parent Consent

All students under 18 must have parent or guardian consent.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

What We Collect

Name, class, school, email, parent email, poem/story text, and UPI Transaction ID. No phone numbers, addresses, or financial details.

How We Use It

  • Verify submission and editorial review
  • Verify UPI payment
  • Contact you about submission status
  • Display published work with attribution

What We Do Not Do

  • Sell information to third parties
  • Use information for advertising
  • Share parent's email
  • Store payment info beyond Transaction ID

Contact

For privacy concerns: hello@wordsvisions.in

Copyright Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Author Retains Copyright

Publishing on this platform does not transfer ownership.

Platform Licence

You grant a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to display, share, and archive your work with full attribution.

Removal Requests

Email hello@wordsvisions.in. Removal processed within 7 working days. Contributions non-refundable upon removal.

Unauthorised Use

Reproducing student work without permission is a copyright violation. Contact us to obtain consent.

Content Moderation Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Editorial Review

Every submission is personally reviewed. No content goes live without approval.

Grounds for Rejection

  • Plagiarism or AI-generated content
  • Violent, sexually explicit, or hateful content
  • Content identifying or endangering a real person
  • Politically inflammatory content
  • Third party copyright violations

Comment Moderation

Comments are moderated. Offensive or spam comments are deleted.

Reporting

Email hello@wordsvisions.in with "Content Report". We respond within 48 hours.
